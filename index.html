A Journey Through Time, The Quest for Understanding

Philosophy from c. 624 BCE to present

Philosophy is a rich and diverse tradition that spans over two thousand years of human history. Starting with the Pre-Socratic philosophers, it has encompassed a wide range of philosophical movements, including the Socratic and Platonic tradition, Hellenistic philosophy, the Aristotelian tradition, Roman and Early Christian philosophy, Medieval philosophy, Renaissance philosophy, Modern philosophy, and Contemporary philosophy. Philosophy has explored a broad range of questions about reality, knowledge, ethics, politics, and the meaning of life, and has made profound contributions to our understanding of the world and ourselves.
